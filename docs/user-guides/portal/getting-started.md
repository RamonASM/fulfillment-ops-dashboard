### Common Questions

**Q: How often should I check the portal?**
A: Once per week is recommended. You'll also receive email alerts for urgent items.

**Q: Can I cancel an order?**
A: Yes, if it's still in "Pending" status. Contact your account manager for approved orders.

**Q: What if the suggested quantity seems wrong?**
A: You can always adjust it. The AI learns from your patterns - if your usage changes, the suggestions will adapt.

**Q: How far in advance should I order?**
A: Check the "Days Remaining" indicator. Order when you reach "Watch" status (15-30 days).

A: Yes! Contact your account manager to add team members.
